

# PIXELORA LABS — Digital Agency Website

A clean, professional, single-page website for a digital agency focused on conversion-centric web design. The site will reflect a calm, confident brand identity with minimalist aesthetics.

---

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Global Header
- Minimal "PIXELORA LABS" text logo
- Navigation links: Home, Services, Work, About, Contact
- Sticky "Get Free Consultation" CTA button that stays visible on scroll
- Mobile hamburger menu for responsive navigation

### 2. Hero Section
- Bold headline: *"Websites that help businesses grow — not just look good"*
- Supporting subtext explaining the agency's approach
- Two CTAs: "Get Free Consultation" (primary teal) + "See Our Work" (secondary outline)
- Subtle abstract gradient background for visual depth

### 3. Who We Help
- 4 clean cards: Local Businesses, SMEs, Startups, Professionals
- Each with a simple icon and 1–2 lines of copy
- Grid layout, responsive to 2 columns on tablet, 1 on mobile

### 4. Services Section
- 3 outcome-focused service cards:
  - Business Websites
  - Conversion-Focused Design
  - Performance & SEO-Ready Builds
- Each card highlights the result/benefit, not just the feature

### 5. Why Pixelora Labs
- Trust-building section with 3–4 key differentiators
- Calm, confident tone — structure, clarity, long-term support
- Clean layout with icons or simple visuals

### 6. Contact Section
- Simple form: Name, Email, Message
- WhatsApp and email contact options displayed alongside
- Clear CTA button to submit

### 7. Footer
- Brand name, navigation links, copyright
- Clean and minimal

---

## Design Direction

- **Theme**: Light mode — white/off-white backgrounds, dark charcoal text
- **Accent colors**: Teal/cyan highlights, soft green/light blue CTAs
- **Typography**: Inter or DM Sans, strong size hierarchy, generous spacing
- **Layout**: Grid-based with ample whitespace, smooth section flow
- **Interactions**: Subtle hover effects on cards and buttons, smooth scroll navigation

---

## Technical Approach

- Fully frontend — no backend needed (form can show a success toast)
- All sections as reusable React components
- Tailwind CSS for styling with custom color tokens
- shadcn/ui components for buttons, cards, inputs, and form elements
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
